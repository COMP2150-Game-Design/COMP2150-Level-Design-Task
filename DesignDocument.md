## 2. Design (~700 words)
Outline and justify how your level design facilitates the core player experience goals outlined in the design brief. Each section should be supported by specific examples and screenshots of your game encounters that highlight design choices made to facilitate that particular experience. Your discussion of design should reference prototypes, playtesting, and any other data that fed into your process.

### 2.1. Discovery
What does the player learn? How does your encounter and broader level design facilitate learning in a way that follows good design practice?

### 2.2. Drama
What is the intensity curve? How does your design facilitate increasing yet modulating intensity, with moments of tension and relief? 

### 2.3. Challenge
What are the main challenges? What type of challenge have you championed and why? How have you designed and balanced these challenges to control the difficulty curve and keep the player in the flow channel?

### 2.4. Exploration
How does your level design facilitate autonomy and invite the player to explore? How do your aesthetic and layout choices create distinct and memorable spaces and/or places?

### 2.5. Reflection
What are some remaining problems in your level design? How would you attempt to tackle these problems in a future iteration? Identification of problems with design and solutions are necessary to receive a D or HD mark.
